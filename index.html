Short Bio

I am a Graduate Research Assistant and M.S. student in the Department of Computer Science at Florida Polytechnic University, advised by Dr. Ayesha S. Dina and Dr. Luis G. Jaimes. My thesis, MA-IDS, proposes a multi-agent RAG framework that integrates LLMs with a FAISS-based experience library for adaptive, explainable IoT network intrusion detection. Before joining Florida Poly, I lectured at Manarat International University and Asian University of Bangladesh, and hold graduate degrees from Jahangirnagar University and Manarat International University.

Research Projects

  • MA-IDS: Multi-Agent RAG Framework for IoT Intrusion Detection 2025 – Present  ·  Thesis

    Multi-agent IDS integrating GPT-4o with a FAISS-based RAG experience library, featuring a Traffic Classification Agent and an Error Analysis Agent for dynamic rule generation. Accepted at IEEE SMC 2026. Advisors: Dr. Ayesha S. Dina, Dr. Luis G. Jaimes.

  • Video-Based Sexual Harassment Detection Using Fine-Tuned Video Transformers 2025 – Present

    Extended the SHVD dataset to 600 annotated clips across indoor and outdoor environments. Fine-tuned VideoMAE, ViViT, and TimeSformer for binary classification; TimeSformer achieved 92.27% test accuracy, outperforming the CNN-LSTM baseline. Accepted at IEEE SMC 2026.

  • PermissionGuard: Fine-Tuned LLMs & BERT for Android Malware Detection 2026 – Present

    Compared LoRA fine-tuning of LLaMA 3.1-8B and full fine-tuning of BERT-base against classical ML baselines for Android permission-based malware detection. Manuscript in preparation.

  • AI Career Counselor & Guidance Platform 2024 – 2025

    LLaMA-powered platform providing personalized IT career roadmaps and labor market insights, trained on an 8,000-point dataset curated from leading educational and job platforms.

  • Real-time Bangla Sign Language Translation System 2022 – 2023

    Transformer architecture for pose-based Bangla sign language video-to-sentence translation. Winner of the national-level "AI for Bangla Competition 2.0" (100+ teams).

  • Detection of Phishing URLs for Secure Browsing 2024

    ML system to detect fraudulent URLs using Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVMs, and Neural Networks with advanced feature selection.

  • Bangla Social Network Post Analysis using Deep Learning 2022

    Transformer-based language model to classify political sentiment and toxicity in real-time Bangla social media posts.

  • Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR) 2018 – 2019

    Real-time ALPR system trained on a Bangladeshi license plate dataset. Published and presented at IEEE RAAICON 2019.
