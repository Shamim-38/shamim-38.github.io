Introduction

I hold the position of a Lecturer in the Computer Science Department at Asian University of Bangladesh. Concurrently, I am engaged as a researcher within the computer vision and machine learning research group. My ongoing research centers around the development of deep learning models tailored for intelligent video surveillance systems. I earned my M.Sc. degree from Jahangirnagar University (JU), where I had the privilege of being mentored by Dr. Mohd. Muzibur Rahman. Furthermore, I attained my B.Sc. from Manarat International University (MIU), guided by the supervision of Sohaib Abdullah.
